Types Of Yoga

Hatha Yoga

This style of yoga focuses on balancing the energy centers and chakras. The energy vortexes or concentrated energy centres in our bodies are known as the chakras.This type of Yoga helps us destress,stay physically fit and agile, and make us look younger

Iyengar Yoga

It emphasizes opening the body up, alignment of the body,and posture in particular.In order to refine positions,it also employs accessories like yoga blocks and belts.One suffers severe neck and back pain in this illness.It aids in the treatment of gastroenterological and gynecological problems.

Kundalini Yoga

kundalini yoga is helpful for stress, anxiety, sadness, and improved cognitive function,which are then followed by pranayama, which is the controlled and precise act of breathing.Jal neti kriya is a purifying method that aids in clearing the nose canal of mucus and other obstructions.

Aerial Yoga

It is appropriate for beginners as well as more experienced users.Aerial yoga,in contrast to traditional yoga,also includes other activities while resisting gravity. It combines the benefits of aerobic exercises like swimming or jogging with yoga.It benefits includes burning fat,it improves flexibility and cures breathing difficulties
